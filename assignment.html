TRAVEL BLOG (Larry's Edition)

There's no doubt that travelling brings you a range of emotions; Joy and excitement, also some form of relief (get-away trip),
but moreso the feeling that you're in an unknown territory. One thing's for sure though;
travelling is probably the best thing you can ever do...

MY TRIP TO SINGAPORE...

The most memorable trip i have ever had was when i was traveling to Singapore alone. Interestingly, it was also my first trip out of my own country. As a child, i wasn't fortunate enough to travel as my parents worked to keep a roof over our heads. It was a Dream of mine since i was very young to go to Singapore. I practically worked my butt off,lol, to fulfill my dream all by myself! I finally got to go in November to see the museum of ice-Cream and also the city's highlight Open-Top Bus. It was a dream come true to see awhole new world and it was really mind blowing! Travelling has made me so humble since then.
